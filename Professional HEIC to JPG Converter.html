About HEIC Format

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is a format used by Apple devices running iOS 11 and later. It offers better compression than JPG but requires conversion for broader compatibility.

This tool converts HEIC images to JPG format, making them compatible with all devices and platforms.
